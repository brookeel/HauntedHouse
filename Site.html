Welcome to Panic Point.

A look into the art of the haunt, the actors, and the crew who make it happen.

Step inside a haunted theme park

and meet the people who make it terrifying.

Panic Point is the highest-rated haunted house in the state of North Carolina, attracting thousands of visitors to its seven outdoor attractions every year. Haunted, directed by Brooke Elliott, explores the lives and passions of the actors, crew, and staff who operate Panic Point on one busy night in October.
